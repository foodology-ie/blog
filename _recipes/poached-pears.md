title: Poached Pears
---

2 pears
250g sugar
1 cinnamon stick
2 cloves
Pinch salt
Lemon zest
400ml red wine
Water to cover

Dissolve sugar in red wine and add in cinnamon, cloves, salt and lemon zest
Peel and core pear
Place the pears in the liquid mixture and top up with water until covered
Simmer the pears until soft
